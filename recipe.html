High-Protein Chicken & Quinoa Bowl

March 10, 2026 · Prep 10 mins · Cook 25 mins · Serves 2

A simple, balanced bowl that's easy to prep ahead and works for lunch or dinner. Packed with lean protein, complex carbs, and plenty of colour — no complicated steps, no hard-to-find ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts (approx. 300g)
  • 150g quinoa (dry weight)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 100g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cucumber, diced
  • Handful of rocket or spinach
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 tbsp tahini (optional)

Nutrition (per serving)

  • Calories520 kcal
  • Protein45g
  • Carbs42g
  • Fat18g
  • Fibre8g

Instructions

  1. Cook the quinoa. Rinse 150g quinoa under cold water. Add to a saucepan with 300ml water, bring to the boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 12–15 minutes until the water is absorbed. Fluff with a fork and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken. Mix the smoked paprika, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Rub the spice mix over both chicken breasts.
  3. Cook the chicken. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken for 6–7 minutes per side until golden and cooked through (internal temp 75°C). Rest for 5 minutes, then slice.
  4. Prep the veg. While the chicken rests, halve the cherry tomatoes, dice the cucumber, and slice the avocado.
  5. Assemble the bowls. Divide the quinoa between two bowls. Top with sliced chicken, avocado, tomatoes, cucumber, and a handful of rocket or spinach.
  6. Dress and serve. Squeeze lime juice over each bowl. Drizzle with tahini if using. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.

Tips

  • Meal prep friendly — stores in the fridge for up to 3 days. Keep the avocado separate until serving.
  • Swap chicken for grilled tofu or chickpeas for a plant-based version.
  • Add a handful of edamame or a soft-boiled egg for extra protein.
